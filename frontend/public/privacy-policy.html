Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Meeting Fatigue Calculator ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google OAuth to access your Google Calendar data. We specifically request the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly scope. This allows us to see your calendar events, their duration, and attendee counts.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your calendar data is used exclusively to calculate your "Meeting Fatigue Score" and provide productivity insights. We categorize meetings using AI (Google Gemini) based on their titles and descriptions.

3. Data Retention (Zero Storage Policy)

We do not store your calendar data. All analysis is performed in real-time within your active session. Once you close the application or your session expires, all retrieved calendar data is purged from our temporary memory. We do not maintain a database of your meetings or personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini AI for meeting categorization. Only meeting metadata (titles/descriptions) is sent to this service. No personally identifiable information is stored by this process.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
