Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Meeting Fatigue Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Meeting Fatigue Calculator, we temporarily access:

  • Google Calendar Data: We read your calendar events from the last 30 days to analyze meeting patterns
  • Google Profile Information: Your name and email address to personalize your report

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your calendar data solely to:

  • Analyze your meeting patterns and calculate fatigue scores
  • Categorize meetings using AI (standups, 1:1s, planning, etc.)
  • Generate personalized insights and recommendations
  • Display your analysis results to you

4. Data Storage and Retention

We do NOT store your calendar data.

  • All analysis happens in real-time when you request it
  • Your calendar data is processed in memory and immediately discarded
  • We do not save, log, or retain any calendar events
  • OAuth tokens are only stored temporarily in your browser session

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Google APIs: To access your calendar (with your explicit consent)
  • Google Gemini AI: Meeting titles are sent to Gemini for categorization (not stored by us)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we process (none is stored permanently)
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Delete: Since we don't store data, closing your browser session removes all traces

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • No persistent storage of sensitive data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Calendar API: To read your calendar events (read-only access)
  • Google Gemini AI: To categorize meeting types
  • Vercel: Frontend hosting
  • Render: Backend hosting

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies for authentication (temporary)
  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Google API Services User Data Policy

Meeting Fatigue Calculator's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
